About Us
 HCH Enterprises, LLC is a New England based company located in Rhode Island. HCH is a small business concern certified by the Small Business Administration and is a participant in the SBA 8(a) program and certified as a small disadvantaged business (SDB). Since 2001 HCH has brought secure information technology, systems management, and voice/data communication, to corporate and governmental institutions. We have provided end-to-end IT solutions, from systems and information assurance, to project planning and management.






History
 The principal IT professionals in our organization have each worked in excess of twenty years in their professions assisting in driving the strategic direction of technology of fortune 100 companies.

HCH Enterprises, LLC was created through the partnership of organizations and information systems professionals within the information technology industry and has been doing business since 1986. The professional relationships built within the industry help us provide best in practice methodologies and the lowest cost solutions to our customers.

These base properties make up our organization and bring over twenty years experience in the delivery of information systems, communications, and security. HCH SME's have extensive experience in the areas of R&D and the implementation of secure systems infrastructures.
