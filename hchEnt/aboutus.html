About Us

HCH Enterprises, LLC was created through the partnership of organizations and information systems professionals within the information technology industry and has been in business since 1986. HCH Enterprises, LLC, was incorporated in June 2001, and certified in information systems security in 2002.

As a SDB Federal Program participant, our mission is to develop and deliver high-value, creative and competitive solutions and services to our government and commercial clients. We strive to cultivate relationships with IT industry leaders in order to provide world class solutions to our customer base. Our Target Markets are small-mid size businesses and corporations, manufacturers, non-profits as well as local state and federal governments offices.

At a Glance

Each of our senior IT professionals at HCH Enterprises bring over thirty years of industry experience to our company. Several staff have also worked at fortune 100 companies and have been the technological driving force for many of today's industry leaders.

HCH Enterprises, LLC brings over thirty years experience in the delivery of information systems, communications, security, program managment, and administrative services. We also have extensive experience implementing systems infrastructure, high bandwidth networks across LANs and WANs. HCH understanding of the connectivity and switching between different network environments strengthen its position as an Integrated Solutions Provider. Since we have customers in the federal, state and private sectors, HCH has accomplished itself as a premier integrator and information technology provider in the private, and government business environments. HCH has been awarded various Federal, State and Local contracts including several with the State of Rhode Island where we migrated legacy applications to reflect current technologies.

While HCH became a part of the State of Rhode Island's MPA program since 2008, we recently completed the Federal 8(a) program in 2013. HCH is currently a part of the Rhode Island Minority Business Enterprise (RIMBE) program and MA State Office of Minority and Women Business Assistance (SOMWBA) program. In our private sector HCH also won several awards. Awards include a 2012 award to transition Sun Healthcare's Virtual Desktop Infrastructure into a new environment and another award from Genesis Healthcare to consolidate company technology at over 180 locations throughout the country.
